The six rulings you are signing off
✓Name: "Quorum" — no "-AI" suffix; the tagline carries the mechanism: Four AI models, one sourced answer. quorum-ai for domains/repos only.
✓Mark: green Q tile + ink wordmark, identical on all surfaces. No other logo colorways.
✓Palette: Light (cool paper #F6F5F2) is the default; warm paper #F2EEE7 is an appearance option; Dark ships behind the ◐ toggle. Ink #14171C · green #0E6B50 · blue #47689E · amber #8A5F16 · red #A83A2A.
✓Green rule: green only means minds agree. One large green surface per journey (the verdict band); small green for consensus/concession/completed; never on buttons, links, or decoration. Money always moves on ink.
✓Layout: one centered reading column everywhere except the live run’s two lanes. Flow is four moments — Ask → Approve the cost → Watch the debate → Verdict — transcript is the only drill-down.
✓Cost transparency: itemized estimate by model AND stage before anything runs; $0.15 confirm / $0.25 hard block; live spend vs approved cap; receipt reconciles est → actual with pipeline timings.

How it works

Ask once. Let four minds argue it out.

Quorum sends your question to four frontier AI models, has them critique each other twice, then hands you one answer that shows where they agree, where they don't, and exactly what to trust.
