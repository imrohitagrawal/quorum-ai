# Acceptance Criteria To Test Map

## Scope

This map links every acceptance criterion to planned test coverage and evidence. It does not claim tests have run. Evidence remains unavailable until implementation and CI produce artifacts.

## Coverage Legend

- Unit: domain/service/component test.
- Integration: database, provider adapter, orchestration, or internal service integration test with stubs.
- Contract: API request/response/error contract test.
- E2E: browser or full workflow test.
- Performance: latency, timeout, cost, or load measurement.
- Security: auth, authorization, redaction, prompt-injection, or abuse test.
- Accessibility: keyboard, screen-reader, focus, label, and contrast verification.
- Eval: AI quality, grounding, high-stakes, citation, or synthesis evaluation.

## Coverage Notes

- Every AC has at least one planned test ID.
- Tests with `N/A` are intentionally not applicable for that test type.
- Security, privacy, and AI safety checks are release blockers even when they are implemented as evals or contract tests rather than traditional unit tests.
- Jira and CI evidence are not claimed until tests are implemented and run.
