# Jira and Confluence Policy

- Use `configs/jira-statuses.json` for all status names and codes.
- Do not move Jira items to `Ready For Dev` until acceptance criteria and test mapping exist.
- Do not move to `CI Validation` without automated checks.
- Do not move to `QA Verified` without QA evidence.
- `Cancelled` and `Duplicate` are terminal.
