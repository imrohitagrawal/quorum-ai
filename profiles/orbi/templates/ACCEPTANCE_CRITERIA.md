# Acceptance Criteria Template

```text
AC-1 (stableKey: <short-kebab-key>, riskTier: Low | Medium | High | Critical):
Given <context>
When <action/event>
Then <expected result>
```

Rules:

- Every AC must be testable.
- Every AC must have a stableKey.
- Every AC must have riskTier.
- Avoid vague words like better, fast, easy, good unless measurable.
